Laptop

The first laptops using the
flip form factor appeared in the early 1980s. The Dulmont Magnum was released in Australia in 1981–82,

but was not marketed internationally until 1984–85. The US$8,150 (equivalent to $24,710 in 2022) GRiD Compass 1101, released in 1982, was used at NASA and by the military, among others.
