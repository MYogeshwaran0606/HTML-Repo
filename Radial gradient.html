Chandrayaan 3 Landed Successfull

PM Modi congratulated Indians and space scientists for the achievement. Chandrayaan-3 Moon Landing Successful: India has created history as it became the first country to land on the South Pole of lunar surface. PM Modi congratulated Indians and space scientists for the achievement.Chandrayaan's maiden mission was in 2008. The Rs 615 crore moon mission was launched on July 14 onboard Launch Vehicle Mark-III (LVM-3) rocket, for a 41-day voyage to reach near the lunar south pole.
